Overreview

Dr. Su is a distinguished specialist in molecular biology and bioinformatics, with extensive experience in the gut microbiome and its relationship to human diseases. He excels in analyzing large multi-omics datasets for both clinical and research applications. As first or corresponding author, he has authored over fifty original research papers in high-impact journals, such as Nature Microbiology, Cell Host & Microbe, Lancet Infectious Diseases, Nature Communications, and Gut. Recognized for his significant contributions, Dr. Su has secured several competitive grants and numerous awards, notably a gold medal from the International Exhibition of Inventions Geneva and Young Investigator Award from KDDW. Additionally, he serves on the editorial board of iMeta and reviews for peer-reviewed journals including Nature Methods, Advanced Science and Gut.
